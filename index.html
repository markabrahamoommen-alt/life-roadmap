Key goal: Build ₹18–22L corpus before IT exit (Feb 2032). Post-IT income from JRF stipend + corpus covers PhD + UPSC prep years in Kerala.
Critical watchpoints
UPSC age deadline
General category age limit is 32 years (Aug 1 cutoff). Last eligible attempt is 2034. Begin by 2029 to use all 6 attempts.
MTech before MBA
Complete MTech first — it is the gateway to PhD enrollment. MBA is a professional add-on, not an academic prerequisite.
Financial corpus
Save ₹18–22L before exiting IT. Post-IT years (UPSC + PhD) need 2–3 years of runway. This is non-negotiable.
Kerala advantage
Relocating to Kerala post-IT drastically reduces living costs. JRF stipend of ₹37–42K/month goes much further there than in Bangalore.
BEd not always needed
For university/college teaching: PhD + NET-JRF is sufficient. BEd is only needed for school (K–12) level teaching.
